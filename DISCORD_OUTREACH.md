## Discord Outreach Workflow

### Step 1: Pre-Launch (Week 1, Mon-Tue)
- [ ] Verify all Discord links are current
- [ ] Join all communities (use Growth team account)
- [ ] Read community guidelines and pinned messages
- [ ] Identify correct channel for post (integrations > general)
- [ ] DM community moderators: "Requesting announcement in [channel] for AI infrastructure survey"

### Step 2: Message Customization
- [ ] Adapt messaging template for each community (personalize to their focus)
- [ ] Keep all messages 150-200 words (Discord readability)
- [ ] Include [LINK] placeholder (filled with Typeform URL on launch)
- [ ] Ensure survey link is shortened (bit.ly / short.link) for mobile

### Step 3: Launch & Engagement (Week 1, Wed-Fri)
- [ ] Post message in all Tier 1 channels (LangChain, Anthropic, OpenClaw)
- [ ] Monitor for replies / questions for 24 hours
- [ ] React to comments, answer questions, thank respondents
- [ ] Repost in Tier 2 communities (Thu-Fri) to maintain visibility

### Step 4: Weekly Follow-Up (Week 2)
- [ ] Post: "Survey extended — we've gotten great feedback so far! [X] developers have responded, thanks!"
- [ ] Tag framework maintainers / community leaders who might amplify
- [ ] Share preliminary findings (framework adoption, top pain points) to build credibility
- [ ] Remind of closing date

### Step 5: Post-Survey (Week 2, Fri)
- [ ] Post final "Survey closed" message
- [ ] Share preliminary results / thank yous
- [ ] Offer: "Interested in early access? Reply here or email [email]"

## Best Practices for Discord Outreach

✅ **DO:**
- Post during peak hours (6-8pm UTC for global communities)
- Engage authentically — answer questions, don't just post and disappear
- Keep messages concise (Discord mobile = limited attention)
- Use format: problem → solution → ask → reward
- Tag relevant channels, not DMs (visibility)
- Share preliminary results (builds momentum)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Spam multiple times per day
- Use aggressive sales language ("Sign up now!" etc.)
- Post identical messages (personalize per community)
- Disappear after posting (respond to comments)
- Forget to thank respondents publicly

## Escalation Path (if engagement is low)

If Discord responses are <10 by mid-Week 1:
1. **Amplify:** Ask maintainers to retweet/share survey to broader community
2. **Add incentive:** Offer $100 Amazon gift card to 5 random respondents
3. **Pivot channels:** Shift focus to HackerNews (typically higher response rate)
4. **Direct outreach:** Email prominent members / framework maintainers directly
