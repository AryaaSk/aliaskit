## Key Messaging (Quick Cheat Sheet)

**Core Positioning:**
"The identity primitive for autonomous agents. One API call, one complete digital identity."

**Tagline:**
"Email, phone, verified identity — without the setup friction."

**3 Positioning Pillars:**
1. **Identity Infrastructure** — Agents are independent entities (not compute extensions)
2. **Developer Experience** — Ship identity in minutes (not weeks)
3. **Bundled & Priced Right** — One provider, one bill, 10x cheaper

**Primary Audience:**
Indie/startup AI engineers building agents (LangChain, Anthropic, OpenClaw users)

**Key Differentiators:**
- Bundled (email + phone, not scattered)
- Agent-optimized (no KYC, simple API)
- Affordable ($50/mo vs $150+ for Twilio)
- Partnership-focused (framework distribution)

**Market Window:**
6-12 months before Twilio/AWS/Azure launch agent-focused offerings. Early partners become standard.
