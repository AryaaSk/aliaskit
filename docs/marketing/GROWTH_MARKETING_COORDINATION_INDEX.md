## Key Decisions & Triggers

**When to escalate to leadership:**
- Any team reports they can't make their deadline
- Multiple teams are behind schedule
- Design assets look significantly different from brief
- Demo fails validation

**When to activate contingencies:**
- Design can't deliver → Use simplified assets
- Demo not ready → Use video demo + early access
- Leadership approves late → Use pre-approved messaging
- GA4 setup fails → Use manual Slack tracking
- Multiple blockers → Activate decision tree

**Go/No-Go decision:**
- GO: All teams ready and systems functioning
- GO with contingencies: Some items at risk but contingencies are deployed
- NO-GO: Critical blocker that can't be fixed in time (recommend delay)
