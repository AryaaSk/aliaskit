## Key Decision Points & Escalation Triggers

### Escalate to Leadership Immediately If:
- Any team can't make their deadline
- Multiple teams are behind schedule
- Critical system is down (API, demo, auth)
- Leadership hasn't reviewed messaging by Day -1 noon
- Support team can't commit to 30-min SLA
- Demo fails validation
- Design assets look significantly different from brief

### Contingency Activation Triggers:
- Design can't deliver → Use simplified assets or templates
- Demo not ready → Use demo video + early access beta
- Leadership approves late → Use pre-approved messaging
- GA4 setup fails → Use manual Slack tracking
- Multiple blockers → Activate decision tree

### Go/No-Go Decision Criteria:
**GO:** All teams ready, systems functioning, confidence high
**GO with contingencies:** Some items at risk but contingencies deployed
**NO-GO:** Critical blocker can't be fixed in time (recommend delay)
